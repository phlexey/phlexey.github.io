Oleksii Korotych is a second-year student at LCC International University in Klaipeda, Lithuania, studying Contemporary Communications. He has been doing photography for more than 7 years now, mostly working as a freelancer. As you can notice in this portfolio, his primary field is fashion photography. Besides, Oleksii does film photography, while being interested in other content-making techniques, such as layout design, video production, etc.

For the past two years, he has been working as a production editor of different yearbooks in the United States and Lithuania. Oleksii possesses great leadership skills, as he has been a part of indefinite number of leadership programs, conferences, events, and other, which makes him a responsible employee/partner, regardless of the workplace. In the future, Oleksii sees himself working as a creative director for a large project-based organization.
